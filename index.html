Questions

Answered directly.

Do you work with solar-only, or solar + battery hybrid installers?

Both. The Growth package is specifically architected around the post-2026 battery rebate, so hybrid installers get the strongest leverage - the payback calculator dynamically includes battery economics where relevant. Solar-only builds still use the same funnel framework without the battery modules.

What happens when STC values step down, or state rebates change?

The calculators are config-driven, not hardcoded. When STC multipliers step down (as they do annually), feed-in tariffs shift, or the battery rebate adjusts, we update the config - your site stays accurate without a rebuild. Enterprise package includes ongoing policy updates as part of scope.

Does this replace or complement competitor / marketplace leads?

Replace, over time. Most installers who work with us gradually reduce marketplace dependency because owned leads are dramatically higher margin - you're not paying $80-$150 per shared lead anymore, and you're not competing against two other quotes on the same job. Some keep a small marketplace spend for volume smoothing during build-up. Your call.

Can it integrate with Open Solar, SolarPlus, or our existing CRM?

Yes. Open Solar, SolarPlus, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, Zoho, and custom setups are all supported. Growth package includes one CRM integration; Enterprise covers multi-system workflows with custom automation.

Who owns the site and source files?

You do. Permanently. Domain, hosting account, source code, design files, CRM data, ad pixels - all registered and built on your accounts from day one. If you ever want to walk away, move providers, or hire internally, nothing breaks.

Is 5-7 days actually realistic for a real solar site?

For Foundation and Growth, yes - because we're not designing from a blank canvas. The conversion architecture is proven, so the build is mostly customisation, content, and integration rather than strategy-from-scratch. Enterprise builds run 10-14 days because of the backend AI and portal work.

Do I need the lead gen system to make this work?

No. The site works standalone. But most installers add managed lead gen within 2-3 months because the conversion infrastructure is already there - you're just turning on the traffic tap. It's optional, not required.
