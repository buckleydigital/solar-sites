The Broker Bottleneck

Every broker hits this wall.

You're a solar broker — your job is to close deals and manage relationships. But instead you're spending half your day answering "what's happening with my system?" calls and chasing installers through WhatsApp threads. That's not a people problem. It's a systems problem. Relay fixes it.

01
Customers in the dark.
They paid $10k+ and have no idea what's happening with their system. So they call you. Every. Single. Day. You're a broker, not a customer service rep.
02
Installers slipping through.
Jobs live in WhatsApp, emails and spreadsheets. Installers miss details. Installations get delayed. Your reputation takes the hit even though it wasn't your fault.

You can't grow past what you can hold in your head. Without a proper operating system, every new job just adds chaos. The ceiling is low and you hit it fast.
